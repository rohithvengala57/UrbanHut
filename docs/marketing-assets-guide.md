# Urban Hut Marketing Assets & Style Guide

## 1. Brand Identity

### Value Proposition
"Live Better, Together."

### Core Pillars
1. **Trust**: Verification that means something.
2. **Compatibility**: Matching based on lifestyle, not just rent.
3. **Harmony**: Built-in tools for shared living (expenses, chores).

---

## 2. Visual Language

### Primary Colors
- **Urban Blue (Primary)**: `#0ea5e9` (Tailwind `primary-500`)
- **Emerald Green (Success/Match)**: `#10b981` (Tailwind `emerald-500`)
- **Slate (Typography/Neutral)**: `#0f172a` (Tailwind `slate-900`)

### Typography
- **Headlines**: Bold, modern sans-serif (e.g., Inter, Montserrat).
- **Body**: Clean, readable sans-serif.

---

## 3. Social Media Copy Bank

### Instagram / TikTok (Short-form Video)
- **Hook**: "POV: You found a roommate who actually cleans the dishes. 🧼"
- **Body**: "Stop gambling with your living situation. Urban Hut uses verified trust scores and lifestyle matching to find your perfect home match. Link in bio to join the waitlist in Jersey City & NYC. #UrbanHut #RoommateGoals #JCRealEstate"

### Reddit (Local Communities)
- **Title**: [Jersey City] Tired of the roommate search struggle?

We focus on identity verification and shared-living tools (expense splitting/chores) built right into the app. We're now live in downtown JC and Manhattan. Check us out if you're looking for a better way to live together! [Link to /city/jersey-city]"

## 4. Landing Page Structure

### Hero
- **Headline**: Live Better, Together.
- **Subheadline**: The all-in-one platform to find compatible roommates, manage shared expenses, and coordinate home life.
- **CTA**: [Get Started] | [Sign In]

### Features
- **Shield Icon**: "Trust First" - Identity verification and transparent trust scores.
- **Users Icon**: "Smart Matching" - Engine finds people who share your values.
- **Credit Card Icon**: "Conflict-Free Living" - Built-in split expenses and chores.

### Social Proof
- "Join 1,000+ early adopters in Jersey City and Manhattan."

## 5. Ad Creative Ideas
1. **The "Split" Graphic**: A split screen showing a messy "Before" (uncoordinated roommate life) and a clean, organized "After" (using Urban Hut).
2. **The "Match" Score**: A stylized graphic showing two profiles with a 95% Compatibility Score, highlighting shared tags like "Quiet at night" and "Loves to cook."
3. **The "Trust" Badge**: A close-up of the "Verified" badge and Trust Score (850) on a user profile.
