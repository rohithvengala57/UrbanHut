# URB-49 Week 1 Acquisition Execution Plan

Prepared: April 27, 2026  
Owner: CMO  
Source approvals: [URB-45](/URB/issues/URB-45)

## 1. Approved Inputs

- Budget envelope (6 weeks): **$7,500**
- Priority cities:
  - **Primary:** Jersey City, NJ
  - **Secondary:** New York, NY (Manhattan)
- Spend governance:
  - CMO can reallocate by channel based on performance
  - Any single partner spend > **$1,500** requires leadership approval

## 2. Week 1 Goals (April 27 - May 3, 2026)

- Launch channel execution in both approved cities
- Generate top-of-funnel demand with attributable UTMs
- Start community/ambassador pipeline for referral-driven signups
- Establish daily operating KPI cadence

Week 1 KPI targets:

- 400 unique landing visitors
- 70 signups
- 35 profile completions
- 20 first-intent actions (`listing_created` or `interest_sent`)

## 3. Week 1 Spend Allocation

Total Week 1 spend: **$1,250** (16.7% of sprint budget)

- Paid social testing (Instagram/TikTok geo-targeted): **$600**
- Creator/partner seeding (micro-creators + community admins): **$300**
- Content production tools + editing support: **$200**
- Contingency for high-performing creative: **$150**

City split:

- Jersey City: **65%**
- Manhattan: **35%**

## 4. Campaign Matrix

UTM convention:

- `utm_source` = instagram | tiktok | reddit | whatsapp | discord | referral
- `utm_medium` = organic | paid | community | creator
- `utm_campaign` = `urbhut_launch_w1_<city>_<theme>`

Week 1 campaigns:

1. `urbhut_launch_w1_jc_trust`
   - City: Jersey City
   - Hook: Safety + trust-led roommate matching
   - Channels: Instagram Reels, Reddit
2. `urbhut_launch_w1_jc_speed`
   - City: Jersey City
   - Hook: "Find compatible roommates fast"
   - Channels: TikTok, WhatsApp groups
3. `urbhut_launch_w1_manhattan_fit`
   - City: Manhattan
   - Hook: Lifestyle-fit roommate discovery
   - Channels: Instagram, Discord housing communities
4. `urbhut_launch_w1_manhattan_cost`
   - City: Manhattan
   - Hook: Shared-living affordability narrative
   - Channels: Reddit + creator collab

## 5. Daily Execution Cadence

Monday (April 27):

- Finalize city-specific creatives (4 hooks x 2 variants)
- Build campaign URLs with UTM schema
- Publish 1 Instagram Reel + 1 TikTok for Jersey City

Tuesday (April 28):

- Launch first paid test set (Jersey City-heavy)
- Publish Reddit posts in 2 city housing communities
- Start outreach to 10 ambassador candidates

Wednesday (April 29):

- Publish Manhattan-focused creative set
- Start WhatsApp/Discord community distribution
- Review day 1-2 funnel metrics and kill low CTR creative

Thursday (April 30):

- Launch best-performing ad variant in both cities
- Publish "roommate safety" educational post for SEO/social crossover
- Follow-up with ambassadors and partner admins

Friday (May 1):

- Publish testimonial-style post and CTA to city landing pages
- Run referral CTA test (value framing A/B)
- Create weekly performance readout draft

Saturday (May 2):

- Community engagement sprint (comments, DMs, warm leads)
- Promote referral content through high-response channels

Sunday (May 3):

- Close week report and recommendations for Week 2 spend shift
- Prepare next week's creative backlog based on winning themes

## 6. Community Execution Checklist

- Build target list of 20 communities:
  - 8 Jersey City housing/student groups
  - 8 Manhattan housing/young professional groups
  - 4 cross-city relocation communities
- Recruit 5 micro-ambassadors (goal: 2 active in Week 1)
- Provide each ambassador:
  - standardized CTA
  - approved claim language
  - tracked referral link

## 7. Measurement and Reporting

Daily metrics to post in [URB-49](/URB/issues/URB-49):

- Spend by campaign
- CTR and CPC by channel
- Signups by campaign/city
- Profile completion rate
- First-intent actions per signup cohort

Decision rules:

- Pause creatives with CTR < 0.8% after 1,000 impressions
- Reallocate 20% of spend toward top campaign every 48 hours
- Keep CAC to activated user trajectory aligned to <= $18 target

## 8. Dependencies and Risk Notes

- Analytics backend work in [URB-42](/URB/issues/URB-42) is in-flight. If backend attribution fields are delayed, maintain UTM capture client-side to keep launch reporting continuous.
- Execution will proceed with UTM discipline and interim spreadsheet tracking so launch momentum is not lost.
