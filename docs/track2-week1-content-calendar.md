## 2. Creative Requirements Checklist

- 4 hook themes: trust, speed, lifestyle fit, affordability.
- 2 variants per hook (8 core short-form assets minimum).
- Include city mention in opening 3 seconds for geo relevance.
- Add explicit CTA and tracked campaign link in every caption.
- Keep trust/safety claims factual and product-verifiable.

## 3. Performance Read Rules

- Evaluate within 24 hours: CTR, thumb-stop rate, watch-through, landing conversion.
- Kill creatives with CTR < 0.8% after 1,000 impressions.
- Reallocate 20% of spend to top-performing campaign every 48 hours.
- Preserve at least one active creative per city/hook family.
